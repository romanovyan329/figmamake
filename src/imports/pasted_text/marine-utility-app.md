Design a modern mobile app for sailors, boat owners, yacht users, and people who regularly go out to sea.
This is a personal marine utility app focused on practical use, trip planning, sea conditions, route preparation, and vessel management.
The app must feel professional, clean, reliable, and useful in real life.

The product is NOT a social app.
No messenger, no chat, no feed, no sharing, no community features.
All data is for personal use only.

The app should combine several core functions in one system:

marine weather and sea conditions

route planning and saved marine points

trip logbook

vessel profile and maintenance tracking

marina / port / anchorage reference

checklists before going to sea

The UX should feel like a mix of:

marine navigation utility

weather planner

vessel management tool

personal trip journal

The interface should be clear, structured, calm, and premium.
Use a nautical visual language without clichés.
Avoid pirate, cartoon, or overly decorative sea themes.
The design should feel serious and trustworthy.

Use a dark marine-inspired palette:

deep navy

ocean blue

slate gray

white / soft light text

accent colors for status:

green for safe / good conditions

yellow for warning / moderate conditions

red for dangerous / not recommended

cyan for interactive active elements

Style:

sleek

minimal

sharp but polished

modern mobile SaaS utility

slightly premium

weather-driven dashboard feel

cards, data blocks, charts, wind widgets, route previews, maps

Important product logic:
The app is built around personal marine preparation and record-keeping.
A user opens the app to quickly understand:

is it safe to go out today?

what are the wind and wave conditions?

what route do I want to take?

which marina / anchorage / point have I saved?

what vessel am I using?

what maintenance or pre-departure tasks should I remember?

what happened during past trips?
